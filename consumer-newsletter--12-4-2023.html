Featured Modality: Osteopathic Medicine
 
 
Osteopathic medicine or osteopathy is a distinct form of Western medicine and the fastest growing form of medicine in the United States. Doctors of osteopathic medicine (DOs or osteopaths) are fully licensed physicians, and practice all types of medicine and surgery. However, osteopathic training emphasizes the idea that the body operates as a connected whole, rather than a collection of separate parts.
